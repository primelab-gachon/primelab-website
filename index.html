News & Updates

Latest achievements and announcements from PRiME Lab

2025.12

Incheon Metropolitan City Award for Bio-Industry Development

Prof. Yong Ho Kim received the Incheon Metropolitan City Commendation for Outstanding Contribution to Bio-Industry Development, recognizing his pioneering work in peptide therapeutics and translational medicine.

2025.11

Incheon SME Grand Prize and Excellence Award

RudaCure Co., Ltd. was honored with the 2025 Incheon Small and Medium Enterprise Grand Prize and selected as an Outstanding Enterprise, highlighting our innovation in peptide drug discovery and commercialization.

2025.08

FDA Phase 2 Clinical Trial Approval for RCI001

RudaCure's dry eye disease drug candidate 'RCI001' has received U.S. FDA approval to proceed to Phase 2 clinical trials, marking a significant milestone in our journey to develop therapeutics leveraging novel TRPV1-associated regulatory mechanisms.

2025.01

Featured in The Scientist: GLP-1 Drugs & Brain Effects

Our research on GLP-1 receptor agonists and their unexplored neurological effects was featured in The Scientist. The article highlights emerging evidence of how weight-loss drugs may impact brain function beyond metabolic regulation. Read more
